About AmpTalent

AmpTalent is a freelance marketplace allowing businesses to find freelancers while simultaneously allowing freelancers to apply for contract positions best suited to their skills at different businesses looking to hire a diverse workforce.

Our company mission is based on Goal #10 REDUCED INEQUALITIES from the UN Sustainable Development Goals
